Our Mission

The coming generation of computing will bring significant changes to every aspect of our lives. Let's make sure we participate in that change. The only way we can do that is to make sure we inspire our students to code.



FAQ

What is VikingsDev?

VikingsDev is a club at Burnaby North Secondary, dedicated to inspiring young students to code. We are a part of Hack Club

Where can I find VikingsDev?

Meetings take place on the third floor of the North Building in Room 315 at Burnaby North Secondary School

When are meetings?

Meetings take place every Wednesday after school. They end at 4:30 pm.

Who can participate?

All current students of Burnaby North Secondary School (BNSS) can join VikingsDev! If you're not a student from BNSS, feel free to drop by and check out what we're doing.
