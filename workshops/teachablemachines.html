Want an AI that can do photo, audio, and pose recognition but don't know where to start? Teachable Machine is your solution; by the end of the workshop, your camera will be able to tell the difference between anything.

Setting Up

All you need for this workshop is a desktop browser and a camera on your device because Teachable Machine runs and does everything in your browser.

Teachable Machine:

Teachable Machine




Upon opening the website, you'll be greeted with a choice on what project you want your AI to be. This workshop will be demonstrating an Image Project, but each step will also work with the other two project types.




Once you have selected your project, you'll find yourself at the training screen. To your left are the classes you can have your AI differentiate. These could be renamed as the item you'll be training. The middle collum is an advance setting you can tweak. You are encouraged to read the information icon beside each option. Lastly, to your right is the preview of how your model is doing.
