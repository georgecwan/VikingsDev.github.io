HackPrep #3 - Twilio 101


LocalHost #4 January 10th 3:15pm to 4:45pm Room 315, BNSS Beginner Friendly

For our third HackPrep, we will be learning how to send SMS messages and phone calls via the Twilio API. At hackathons, there's a limited amount of time to create your projects, this means you should use as many APIs as possible. This workshop will help you learn the process of using an API and integrating it within your own app.

HackPrep is a series of workshops we will be running to get you ready for hackathons, such as nwHacks 2020. We will be running three HackPreps in the next two months, stay tuned!
